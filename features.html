Features that elevate

Get your own phone number

Every organization on evant gets a unique US phone number. Your organization can request to change to a short code, change the area code or change the country of origin.

Easy Control

Organization admin can create events through sms. No need to login on the website.

Paid Events

All plans other than the free plan include paid events. Payments are powered through stripe.

Tiered Event Pricing

Create tiers for events, or support member and non member pricing. Evant gives you the ability to have control how your events are priced.

Expand your communication avenues.
evant can support whatsapp and facebook messenger.

CRM

evant can be integrated with salesforce for all business accounts. With an enterprise account evant can plugin to your CRM of choice.

Virtual Events

Virtual events do not require any video software to be installed for your customers. There are three types of virtual events, livestream, panel, and cocktail party. A livestream has one host broadcasting to all the organization members. A panel has up to four broadcasters and a cocktail party is a networking event where members can meet with other members on one-on-one video calls and share contact info.

Ticketing

evant supports QR code ticket generation. Members will get sent a QR code after they sign up for an event for use on entry to the event.

Dashboard

Admins can create events, coordinate who is going, and communicate with their members all through the dashboard.

Import Current Users

Import your existing customer base onto evant.

Custom Sign Up Flow

evant can have a unique signup flow for your organization. If you need non conventional pieces of information from your community members, like a picture of their id evant can be tailored for that.
